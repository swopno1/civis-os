# Milestone 00: Traction Blitz (Weeks 1-2)

## Goal
Establish the initial brand presence and prove the "Digital Lifeboat" concept to the early-adopter community. Focus on organic reach through high-impact messaging.

---

## 📢 Social Media & Marketing (SMM)
**Strategy:** "The Manifesto Blitz" - Position CivisOS not just as software, but as a survival necessity.

- **Tasks:**
  - Draft and publish the "Digital Lifeboat Manifesto" on X (Twitter), Reddit (r/selfhosted, r/preppers, r/opensource), and Hacker News.
  - Launch "Daily Survival Tips" series: 14 days of short-form content showing how CivisOS solves specific crisis scenarios (e.g., "How to chat when the towers are down").
  - Create a "Waitlist" or "Early Adopter" role in a new Discord/Matrix community.
- **Achievable Target:**
  - 1,000 Unique Visitors to the Landing Page.
  - 100 GitHub Stars.
  - 50 Community Members (Discord/Matrix).

---

## 📦 Product Development
**Strategy:** Landing Page Optimization & First Impression.

- **Tasks:**
  - Deploy the "Tracing-Free" Analytics on the landing page to track conversion.
  - Optimize Landing Page SEO for keywords: "Decentralized OS", "Off-grid Mesh", "Survival Tech".
  - Ensure 100% PWA Lighthouse score for mobile "Add to Home Screen" experience.
- **Achievable Target:**
  - < 2s Page Load Time on 3G connections.
  - 20% Conversion Rate (Visit to "Boot OS" click).

---

## 🛠️ Developers & Engineering
**Strategy:** Stability & Onboarding.

- **Tasks:**
  - Finalize `CONTRIBUTING.md` with a "Good First Issue" list.
  - Ensure the Web-Serial mesh bridge is plug-and-play for standard ESP32 boards.
  - Implement a basic "Bug Bounty" recognition system (hall of fame).
- **Achievable Target:**
  - 0 Critical "Boot-Loop" bugs reported.
  - 5 External Pull Requests (even for docs).
