# Financial Estimates & Revenue Model

This document outlines the lean bootstrapping budget required to launch CivisOS and the conservative "Grassroots" revenue model projected for Years 2 and 3.

## 1. Launch Cost Estimate (Lean Bootstrapping)

Calculated for 1 lead developer and 2 part-time contributors over 1 year to reach a fully functional Phase 3.

| Item | Estimated Cost (USD) | Notes |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Development Stipends** | $24,000 | 1 year for core dev (lean/stipend-based). |
| **Infrastructure** | $1,200 | Neutral hosting in Switzerland/Iceland. |
| **Hardware R&D** | $3,000 | Prototype kits (ESP32, e-Ink screens, LoRa modules). |
| **Security Audit** | $5,000 | Critical for "Global Trust" (Open-source audit). |
| **Legal/Foundation** | $2,000 | Registering as a Swiss Non-Profit or DAO. |
| **TOTAL** | **$35,200** | **To reach a fully functional Phase 3.** |

## 2. Revenue Estimation (Year 2 - Year 3)

We assume a conservative "Grassroots" adoption model, aiming to cover server costs, global hardware distribution, and 2-3 full-time developer salaries without relying on venture capital.

### A. Hardware Shop (The Civis-Node)
* **Item:** Pre-configured LoRa Survival Node.
* **Price:** $65 (Cost: $40, Profit: $25).
* **Target Volume:** 2,000 units/year.
* **Estimated Annual Revenue:** $50,000 profit.

### B. Community Verified Badges
* **Target:** Local neighborhood leaders and "Safe Zone" organizers.
* **Price:** $1/month.
* **Target Volume:** 5,000 verified nodes globally.
* **Estimated Annual Revenue:** $60,000.

### C. B2B / NGO Modules
* **Target:** Small NGOs or local municipal disaster departments.
* **Licensing:** $500/year for advanced logistics/inventory modules.
* **Target Volume:** 50 organizations.
* **Estimated Annual Revenue:** $25,000.

### D. Open Collective / Bounties
* **Target:** Community-funded features.
* **Estimated Annual Revenue:** $10,000.

### Total Estimated Annual Revenue (Year 3): ~$145,000
